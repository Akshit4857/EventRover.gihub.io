FAQs

Have Questions? We're here to help.

What is EventRover?

EventRover is a platform dedicated to helping you discover hackathons organized by various colleges. It also offers a unique rewards system where you can earn points for posting events.

How do I find hackathons on EventRover?

Simply visit the homepage and use the search or browse features to find hackathons by college, date, or other criteria./h4>

How can I earn rewards on EventRover?

You can earn rewards by posting hackathon events. Each event you post earns you points that you can later redeem for merchandise or event passes.

How do I post a hackathon event?

Log in to your account, navigate to the "Post an Event" section, and fill in the required details about the hackathon you want to promote.

How do I redeem my earned points?

Go to your profile, find the "Redeem Points" section, and choose your preferred reward. Follow the instructions to complete the redemption.

Is there a limit to the number of events I can post?

No, you can post as many hackathon events as you'd like. However, each event must be unique and follow our guidelines.

How can I contact EventRover support?

If you have any questions or issues, please reach out to us through the "Contact Us" page, and we'll be happy to assist you.
